Welcome to CMCS!

Central Mindanao Computer School (CMCS) stands as a distinguished institution in the realm of technology education, proudly situated on the 2nd floor of Villasor Building, Burgos Street, Mlang, Cotabato. With an impressive 27-year legacy, CMCS was founded by the visionary Jonathan Abasques, marking a commitment to providing quality education in the ever-evolving field of information technology.

Our esteemed institution offers a robust Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) program, a comprehensive 2-year course meticulously designed to impart the latest industry knowledge and skills. Complementing this, our Associate in Computer Technology (ACT) program provides a focused 2-year curriculum aimed at cultivating practical expertise.

Nestled in the vibrant community of Mlang, CMCS not only provides a conducive learning environment but also fosters a corporate culture that encourages innovation and collaboration. At CMCS, we pride ourselves on delivering a blend of academic excellence and hands-on experience, ensuring our students emerge as adept professionals ready to navigate the complexities of the digital landscape.

As a beacon of quality education, CMCS remains steadfast in its dedication to shaping the future of our students, preparing them to meet the demands of the corporate world with confidence and competence.

Mission


To develop an individual through education based on TRUTH, KNOWLEDGE, TECHNOLOGY and HARDWORK.
To give meaningful education as manifested in the exercise of Christian Education as a part of sound professional technical training for community services.
To provide every individual an opportunity to education and develop his talents and gifts as a person.

Vission


To become partner in community development and nation-building through education, employment, livelihood products and early development of the youth with exemplary interest and capabilities.
